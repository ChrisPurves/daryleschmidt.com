Teaching Positive Ways To Get Positive Results

Hi, my name is Daryle Schmidt. I own and run Daryle Schmidt Dog Training Centre which is located 3 miles north of Drayton Valley, Alberta and 3.5 miles east of Highway #22. If you travel through Rocky Rapids continue 2.5 miles east of the Rocky Rapids Store and you will find us on the south side of the road.

I'm offering a variety of services which include: one on one lessons teaching Basic Dog Obedience, I provide a service when you are on holidays to train your dog while your dog is boarded here, a walking / training service, and I also train working stock dogs. My methods are the latest in dog psychology training. I teach people to understand how their dog thinks and why they react in a certain manner.

You can come to my training centre for lessons or I can come to your home. If your dog needs additional training to over come engrained behaviours and habits I can take your dog to my training centre until he is ready to return home.

Together as a group effort I can help you solve problems by teaching you positive ways to get positive results. I will show you how to correct unwanted behaviour and when to give praise.
